<objective>
Close the engine-side code-review findings in `treelite-gtil` that violate the 1e-5 cast-order contract or the ERR-01 "malformed Model never panics / never silently mis-predicts" contract: CR-01 (f64-input postprocessors collapsed to f32), WR-02 (shape/predict third-dim clamp disagreement), WR-03 (node-0 OOB on a 0-node tree), WR-04 (malformed category-list/leaf-vector offsets silently change the prediction), WR-05 (`kNone` on a numerical node silently routes right).

Purpose: CR-01 is the headline 1e-5 fidelity defect — the f64 × {sigmoid, exponential, exponential_standard_ratio, logarithm_one_plus_exp, signed_square, multiclass_ova} surface ships wrong-precision output, masked only because the current f64 sigmoid goldens sit ~1e-7 inside band. WR-02…WR-05 harden the malformed-model attack surface so a corrupt `Model` produces a typed `GtilError`, never an OOB panic or a definite wrong routing. The dependent large-margin f64 fixture and harness assertions that PROVE CR-01 land in Plan 05-07 (next wave).
Output: O-generic postprocessor body (softmax stays f32), agreeing shape clamps, node-0 guard, typed errors for malformed CSR offsets and unrecognized operators, all new variants in `error.rs`.
</objective>
