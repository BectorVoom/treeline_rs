Complete the postprocessor surface (the 3 remaining: `signed_square`, `hinge`, `multiclass_ova`) and the full categorical float-representability guard + child polarity — both ported verbatim from upstream — so the GTIL reference matches upstream on all 10 postprocessors (GTIL-04) and on edge categorical values (GTIL-06). </objective>
